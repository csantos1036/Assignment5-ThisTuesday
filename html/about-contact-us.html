The Backbone of This Tuesday About Our Store

Beggining in 2007, at This Tuesday, we firmly believe in purchasing and reselling secondhand items, such as clothing, furniture, and glassware. The goal is to limit our waste by reusing quality, vintage items in our homes and lifestyle. There are many benefits to purchasing secondhand. It is a lot more sustainable and eco-friendly. There's also an excitement and joy involved with looking and purchasing items that no one else will have, unlike shopping at fast fashion and big box stores. We appreciate all our customers who have supported us along the way.
